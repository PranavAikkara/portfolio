Data Science in the Age of Agentic AI

Data professionals might feel a bit disoriented these days. New agentic tools keep arriving and generating buzz. Concerns around AI replacing human-filled roles keep growing — including in areas deemed safe not so long ago.

 

What does the future hold for data scientists, engineers, and analysts? How can they adapt to a rapidly changing landscape for hiring and career growth? What does excelling mean for a data practitioner when an AI agent can likely accomplish in seconds tasks that used to take days?

 

This week's highlights offer sharp insights, actionable advice, and a healthy (if nuanced) sense of optimism.

Beyond Code Generation: AI for the Full Data Science Workflow. 

 Instead of worrying about being displaced by AI, Yu Dong suggests data scientists should find smart and effective ways to harness it across the entire data pipeline. "The bigger shift," Yu argues, "is that AI can now participate in a much more end-to-end data science workflow."
