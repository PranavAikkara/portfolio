How to Keep Your Agentic Workflows Sustainable

AI powerhouses make money when their customers burn through as many tokens as possible, as quickly as possible. End users, on the other hand, often don't have a clear idea of how sustainable (or wasteful) their work habits are.

 

Combine these two statements, and you end up with sprawling, costly pipelines, overly complex systems, and poor ROI. Sounds familiar?

 

If you or your team want to reign in your usage, produce better results, and establish a tighter grip on resources — financial, logistical, or environmental — read on to explore this week's lineup of standout articles.
