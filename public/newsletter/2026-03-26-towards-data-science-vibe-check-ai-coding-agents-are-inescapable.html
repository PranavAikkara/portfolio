Vibe Check: AI Coding Agents Are Inescapable

Tools like Claude Code are reshaping workflows and job descriptions, making some tasks a breeze while complicating the way many of us think about technical expertise. That's true whether you're an early adopter or a holdout, a cheerleader or a skeptic.

 

What does it mean to be a developer, product builder, data analyst, or ML engineer in a world in which fewer and fewer people actually write code from scratch? What do we stand to gain by this transformation — and what risks are we taking on along the way?

 

This week, our authors offer nuanced perspectives on what is quickly becoming the defining phenomenon of a new tech landscape.

 

The New Experience of Coding with AI

Coming to the topic from the perspective of someone with a long career in data science and ML, Stephanie Kirmer reflects on the dramatic changes we're seeing in the wake of coding assistants' rise. Does it feel like you can continue working in software engineering and not pick up these tools?"
